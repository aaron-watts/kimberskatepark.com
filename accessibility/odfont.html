Accessibility Statement

Kimber Skatepark is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

Conformance Status

The Kimber Skatepark website conforms to WCAG 2.2 Level AA. This means we meet the mid-range level of accessibility requirements, which is the standard most widely referenced by accessibility laws.

Third-Party Content

Scheduled events are collected from external services, and may not meet Accessibility Standards.
